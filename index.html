2023

In the aftermath of a devastating cyclone in February 2023, our local community faced significant hardship.

Recognizing the need for support, I initiated a fundraising campaign printing t-shirts and other merchandise with artwork designed by a local artist specifically for the fundraiser to assist affected residents.

The campaign was a resounding success, raising over $30,000. This initiative not only provided much-needed relief to those in need but also significantly boosted our business's customer base and strengthened our community ties.
